About the Role
As a Sales Development Representative at Exa, you will build high quality pipeline across enterprise and developer segments. You will own outbound prospecting, qualify inbound interest, and partner closely with Account Executives to turn product interest into revenue.

This role is fully in office in San Francisco. We believe working together in person drives speed, accountability, and learning.

We are looking for competitive, curious, high ownership individuals who want to help build a category defining AI company.

What You Will Do
Generate pipeline through personalized outbound across email, phone, and social

Engage developers, founders, and enterprise leaders in thoughtful conversations

Write clear, compelling messaging tailored to AI, search, and infrastructure use cases

Partner with AEs, Marketing, and founders on targeted outbound campaigns

Build and prioritize account lists within high value segments

Qualify inbound leads and convert product interest into meetings

Conduct high level conversations with technical and executive stakeholders

Hit and exceed monthly quotas for meetings, opportunities, and pipeline generated

Track performance metrics and continuously improve outreach strategy

Contribute to outbound playbooks and messaging frameworks

What We Are Looking For
1+ years of sales experience, ideally in SaaS, AI, infrastructure, or high growth tech

Strong interest in AI, developer tools, or technical products

Competitive mindset with a strong internal drive to win

Comfortable in a fast moving, high performance environment

Resilient and coachable

Clear and concise communicator

Strong organizational skills and ability to prioritize

Self starter mentality. When you see an opportunity, you act
