About the Role

As an Account Executive at Exa, you will work with customers who need speed, intelligence, and accuracy in their workflows. You’ll take full ownership of deals—prospecting, running demos, negotiating, and closing.

What You’ll Do

Own the entire sales cycle—from first touch to close

Run product demos tailored to customer asks & unique use cases

Develop and execute strategies to land high-value customers

Work closely with SDRs to keep the pipeline strong and moving

Negotiate contracts, hit targets, and drive revenue growth

Who You Are

2+ years in a sales role, ideally in SaaS or AI/tech products

Experience managing a full sales cycle, from lead generation to close

Strong communication skills—you can tell a great story and sell a vision

A consultative approach—help customers see the business value, not just the features

A competitive edge—you’re driven, persistent, and love closing deals

Comfort in a fast-moving, high-growth startup environment
